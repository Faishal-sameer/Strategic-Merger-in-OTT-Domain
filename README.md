# Strategic-Merger-in-OTT-Domain
OTT data analysis project using SQL to generate business insights and strategic recommendations.


📊 OTT Platform Merger Analysis (LioCinema & Jotstar)
🚀 Project Overview

This project analyzes a potential merger between two OTT platforms — LioCinema and Jotstar — with the objective of identifying key business insights and strategic opportunities.

The focus is not just on SQL analysis, but on solving real-world business problems such as low user conversion, high inactivity rates, and revenue imbalance in the OTT industry.

🧩 Business Problem

The OTT industry in India is experiencing rapid growth, but platforms face critical challenges:

High user acquisition but low conversion to paid users
Increasing inactive users (churn risk)
Imbalance between user growth (scale) and revenue generation (monetization)

👉 Key Question:
How can a merger between LioCinema and Jotstar maximize growth, engagement, and revenue?

🎯 Objectives
Analyze platform performance (Jan–Nov 2024)
Understand user behavior and engagement patterns
Identify upgrade/downgrade trends
Evaluate revenue distribution across user segments
Provide data-driven business recommendations
📊 Dataset Description

The dataset includes:

User information (age group, city tier, subscription plan)
Content data (type, language)
Watch time and engagement metrics
Subscription activity (upgrade, downgrade, inactivity)
Revenue-related data
🛠️ Tools & Technologies
SQL (Data extraction & analysis)
Data Cleaning & Transformation
Business Analysis & Insight Generation
📈 Key Analysis Performed
1. User Growth Analysis
Compared total users across both platforms
Identified growth trends over time
2. User Segmentation
Analyzed users by:
Age group
City tier (Tier 1, 2, 3)
Subscription plan
3. Engagement Analysis
Evaluated watch time behavior
Compared active vs inactive users
4. Subscription Behavior
Identified upgrade and downgrade patterns
Analyzed user journey from free → paid plans
5. Revenue Analysis
Calculated total revenue
Compared monetization efficiency across platforms
🔥 Key Insights

In a highly competitive OTT market, growth without monetization is unsustainable. The analysis reveals that combining scale with engagement can unlock exponential value.

LioCinema drives high user acquisition but has lower engagement
Jotstar has higher watch time and revenue per user
Low watch time users are more likely to become inactive (churn risk)
Tier 2/3 cities contribute to growth, while Tier 1 cities drive revenue
Most users follow a gradual upgrade path, not direct premium conversion
💡 Business Recommendations
1. Improve User Conversion
Introduce step-based pricing models
Offer trial-based premium plans
2. Reduce Churn (Inactivity)
Implement re-engagement campaigns
Use personalized content recommendations
3. Increase Engagement
Promote binge-worthy content
Enhance UI/UX for content discovery
4. Tier-Based Pricing Strategy
Affordable plans for Tier 2/3 cities
Premium-focused offerings for Tier 1 users
5. Optimize Content Strategy
Combine regional reach (LioCinema) with premium content (Jotstar)
🎯 Final Conclusion

The merger between LioCinema and Jotstar presents a strategic opportunity to combine scale with monetization. While LioCinema brings a large user base, Jotstar contributes higher engagement and revenue efficiency. By focusing on user conversion, engagement, and retention, the combined platform can establish a dominant position in the OTT market.
